Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Account information: Username, email address, and a securely hashed password.
  • Social platform tokens: OAuth access and refresh tokens when you connect your social media accounts (Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, Facebook).
  • Content data: Posts, drafts, schedules, and media you create or upload through the Service.
  • Analytics data: Engagement metrics retrieved from your connected social media platforms.
  • Integration tokens: Telegram bot tokens and chat IDs if you enable Telegram notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service, including publishing content and retrieving analytics.
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • Enhance content using AI providers (OpenAI, Google Gemini) -- your content prompts are sent to these services for processing.
  • Send notifications via Telegram if you have configured this integration.
  • Improve the Service through aggregated, anonymized usage patterns.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database hosted on Neon with encrypted connections.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plaintext.
  • OAuth tokens are stored encrypted and used only for API calls on your behalf.
  • We use HTTPS for all data transmission.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Twitter / X -- for content publishing and analytics retrieval.
  • LinkedIn -- for content publishing and engagement tracking.
  • YouTube (Google) -- for video publishing and analytics.
  • TikTok -- for video publishing and metrics.
  • Facebook / Instagram -- for content publishing.
  • OpenAI -- for AI content generation and enhancement.
  • Google Gemini -- for AI content generation and enhancement.
  • Telegram -- for bot-based notifications.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data is shared only with:

  • Third-party APIs listed above, solely to provide the Service functionality you requested.
  • Law enforcement, if required by law or legal process.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data associated with your account.
  • Correct: Update your profile information at any time.
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Disconnect: Remove any connected social media platform from your dashboard.
  • Export: Request a copy of your data in a portable format.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data will be removed within 30 days. Anonymized analytics data may be retained for service improvement purposes.

8. Cookies

The Service uses JWT tokens stored in browser localStorage for authentication. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through the Service. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@kontentpyper.com.
